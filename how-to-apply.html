Things To Know First

Students should come with a notebook, a pen or pencil, and if possible, a personal laptop or tablet. However, we do have a limited number of devices available for those who may not have one.

Your child can join even after the session has started. Our instructors will provide extra support to help them catch up on any missed content.

Certifications & Refund Policy

At the end of the program, students will receive a Certificate of Participation. Exceptional students may receive special recognition or project-based badges.

  • Certificate of Participation for all who complete the cycle.
  • Recognition badges for standout students and projects.
  • Partial refund available if you cancel within the first 2 weeks.
  • No refunds after the first 2 weeks due to planning and resources.
  • All classes are held in a safe, structured learning environment.
