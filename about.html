About Us

Our Learning System Inspires Young Minds

At Cyan Nextgen Tech Academy, we help kids and teens explore the exciting world of technology early. Through hands-on learning, they build skills in coding, robotics, and creative problem-solving—laying a strong foundation for future success in tech.

Practical Tech Education

We offer beginner-friendly lessons in coding, game dev, and robotics using fun, real-world projects.

Future-Ready Skills

Our students gain skills in tech, teamwork, and problem-solving—skills they’ll use anywhere in life.

“Children don’t just learn tech here—they build the confidence to create, lead, and thrive in the digital world.”
